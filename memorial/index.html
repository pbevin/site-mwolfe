Menya's Memorial Service

We held Menya's memorial service on Sunday March 2, 2001 at Hart House, University of Toronto. Nearly 400 people came to celebrate Menya's life.

To listen to these pieces, you may need to download a free MP3 player for your computer. See http://www.help.mp3.com/help/player.html for recommended software.

  • Farewell to Stromness (3.6Mb) - Jennifer Wolfe, piano
